Blast Galaxy is the ultimate destination for groups and companies looking for... a day of classic arcade fun. We have the traditional experience in a modern one arcade club reimagined, while being the soul and spirit of the classic arcade maintain a great atmosphere and a high quality and creative menu combine with a unique selection of over 100 great arcade games.

We're closed to the general public several days a week, allowing you to have the place all to yourselves. The perfect setting for a group to enjoy our food and drinks, and all our arcade games, either at your own pace of guided by our team with a fun tournament. Blast Galaxy also serves as a one-of-a-kind backdrop for any event such as press and launch events, video shoots, or esports compitions and offer a private area for presentations and meetings.
